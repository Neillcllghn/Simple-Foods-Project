How to make Vegetarian Lasagne

Recipe Details:

  • Serving: 4
  • Prep time: 30-40 mins
  • Cooking time: 40 mins
  • Utensils needed: Sauce-pans, Casserole-dish

Ingrediants:


  • Quorn Mince 350g: Tesco - €3.50

  • Tesco Extra Olive Oil 500ml: Tesco - €2.49

  • Dunnes Stores Lasagne Sheets: Dunnes - €0.60

  • Dairygold Dairyfree Cheese: Dunnes - €3.50

  • Dolmio Tomato Red Sauce: Tesco - €3.09

  • Tesco White Sauce: Tesco - €0.99

Instructions:


  1. Preparing the mince (meat-free):

    Take out the sauce-pan. Pour in some Olive Oil to wet the base of the sauce-pan before adding the Quorn Mince (2 Packs).

  2. Heating the mince (meat-free):

    Turn on the oven hub (set the temp between 4-6) and place sauce-pan the on the hub. Stir the mince regulary to brown it (adding olive oil when the base appears dry).

  3. Add the tomato Sauce:

    Once the mince is sufficiently cooked (should only take between 2-3 mins), add the Dolmio Tomato Sauce. Mix it in thoroughly while it is still on hub for 10 mins.

  4. Lasagne Sheets:

    Take out the casserole-dish, add in small amount of olive oil before placing the Lasagne sheets to cover the entire base.

  5. Add the prepared mince:

    Add in the mince from the sauce pan, only enough to cover base of lasagne sheets. Place more lasagne sheets to cover the mince.

  6. Add the white sauce:

    Now add the white sauce to cover the second layer of lasagne and then repeat point 5 above.

  7. Add the dairy-free cheese:

    Now that you have used up all the mince, layer the top with lasagne sheets and then sprinkle the grated cheese (add in as much to cover the entire top).

  8. Turn on oven:

    Preheat oven: 180c/fan/160c/gas 4 and leave preheat for 10 - 15mins before placing the casserole-dish in. Once ready, leave lasagne for 30-40 mins.

  9. Serve:

    Congradulations, you have successfully prepared your Vegetarian Lasagne!
